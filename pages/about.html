About

Hi, my name is Oli and I want to make to make web apps that are awesome for everyone.

For the last two years I have worked on a project called digital inclusion, which aims to teach essential skills and provide opportunities for people that have been excluded from technology.

The project got me working with computers, mobile phones and the internet every day. There was something magical about showing people what they could do with modern tech. I wanted to make a website to publicly store resources for coaching work and this led me to build my first website using Wix. It was this experience that led me to the world of HTML, CSS, JavaScript and ultimately to where I am now in my coding journey.

Throughout the project i've gained an acute awareness that many web and software applications fail the very people that could benefit from such technologies the most. I want to change that and build software that is useful, fast and enjoyable for all.

Check out my Inclusive development page to see some examples of inclusive web development.

The second focus of my application site is 'mental health', a topic that I really care about and an area I have been fortunate to work in over the last three years.

Check out my Digital health page to find a variety of digital mental and physical health tools built with JavaScript.

I believe that I'm a good fit for the Founders and Coders programme because I've come to realise through training, studying and working that the times I have been most motivated, engaged and resilient have been the times when I truly felt part of a group. I enjoy explaining and exploring code with others just as much as learning, following and building. The coaching groups have given me insight into the co-operative and collaborative ethos of the programme and has helped me build the confidence to bring my experience and values to the web developer developer community.
