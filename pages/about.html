About me

Hi, my name is Oli and I want to make web apps that are awesome for everyone.

For the last three years I have worked on a community project called digital inclusion, which aims to teach essential skills and provide opportunities for people that have been excluded from technology.

The project got me working with computers, mobile phones and the internet every day. There was something magical about showing people what they could do with modern tech. During the pandemic I wanted to make a website to publicly store resources for coaching work and this led me to build my first website using Wix. It was this experience that led me to the world of HTML, CSS, JavaScript and ultimately to where I am now in my coding journey.

Throughout the project i've gained an acute awareness that many web and software applications fail the very people that could benefit from such technologies the most. I want to change that and build software that is useful, fast and enjoyable for all.

Check out my Inclusive development page to see some examples of inclusive web development in practice.

The second focus of my application site is 'mental health', a topic that is very important to me. As part of my public involvement in research work at the McPin Foundation I have seen a flurry of development in the field of technology for mental health and wellbeing, including a mobile app to help with paranoia, and a virtual reality therapy for psychosis. I would love to develop in this space in the future using the skills from the apprencticeship to create quality software that has an impact.

Check out my Digital health page to explore a digital health tool I built with JavaScript.

I believe that I'm a good fit for the Founders and Coders programme because I've come to realise through training, studying and working that the times I have been most motivated, engaged and resilient have been the times when I truly felt part of a group. I enjoy explaining and exploring code with others just as much as learning, following and building. The coaching groups have given me insight into the co-operative and collaborative ethos of the programme and has helped me build the confidence to bring my experience and values to the web developer developer community.
